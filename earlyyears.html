Early Years

This page shows demographics of early years of John Patrick Quing


John Patrick Quing was born on 20th August 1984 at Mary Chiles Hospital Sampaloc Manila Philippines.John Patrick's father is a civil engineer, and his mother is a nurse but worked as a factory worker. He has a lovely younger sister Princess Margarette Quing and youngest brother Prince Charles Quing. When he was 2 years old, Manila is under siege, and his parents decided to let his grandparents to take care with him in provice of Sta. Cruz, Marinduque. After 2 years his parents get him back from his grandparents.
John Patrick was a hyperactive and playful boy. He has a lot friends from his neighbor.
